Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains how Pontora collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Pontora is a service connecting local people with university students for everyday tasks. The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

  • Name: William Durrant
  • Trading as: Pontora
  • Email: [email protected]
  • Website: pontora.co.uk

2. What Data We Collect

When you sign up to our waitlist, we collect the following personal data:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Date and time of sign-up

We do not collect any sensitive personal data, payment information, or data from children under the age of 13.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your data for one purpose only:

  • To notify you when Pontora launches in your area — we will send you a one-time SMS and email when the app goes live. That is the sole reason your data is collected.

The legal basis for processing your data is consent (Article 6(1)(a) UK GDPR). By submitting your details via our waitlist form, you consent to us sending you a single launch notification.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data will only be used to contact you about the Pontora launch. We will not:

  • Sell your data to any third party
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Use your data for any purpose other than those stated in this policy

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We may share your data with the following trusted service providers solely to operate the waitlist:

  • Render (render.com) — our hosting provider, where your data is stored securely
  • SMS provider — used to send you a launch notification text message (e.g. Textlocal)
  • Email provider — used to send you a launch notification email (e.g. Mailchimp)

All third-party providers are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with UK GDPR.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We will retain your waitlist data until one of the following occurs:

  • You request that we delete your data
  • Pontora launches and the waitlist is closed (at which point waitlist data will be deleted within 30 days of launch)
  • 12 months have passed since you signed up, whichever comes first

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct any inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us
  • Right to complain — you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected] and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Render, which uses industry-standard encryption and security practices. Access to your data is restricted to William Durrant only.

9. Cookies

Our waitlist page does not use any cookies or tracking technologies. We do not use Google Analytics or any other tracking tools on this page.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
